## Phase 1: Opening · 120s
<!-- phase_id: 1, planned_duration_seconds: 120 -->

### Beat: Hook
<!-- beat_id: 1_hook, tags: [critical, joke] -->
Open with a surprising story or statistic that grabs attention immediately.
Counts as covered when the speaker delivers the opening and the audience reacts.

### Beat: Credibility
<!-- beat_id: 1_cred, tags: [critical] -->
One sentence on why you are the right person to talk about this topic.
Counts as covered when the speaker establishes a personal connection to the subject.

## Phase 2: Problem · 180s
<!-- phase_id: 2, planned_duration_seconds: 180 -->

### Beat: Problem Statement
<!-- beat_id: 2_problem, tags: [critical] -->
Name the specific pain point clearly. What is broken or missing today?
Counts as covered when the speaker names the concrete problem.

### Beat: Evidence
<!-- beat_id: 2_evidence -->
Back the problem with data, a story, or an audience poll.
Counts as covered when a concrete example or number is given.

### Beat: Stakes
<!-- beat_id: 2_stakes, tags: [critical] -->
Why does this matter? What happens if the problem goes unsolved?
Counts as covered when the speaker explains the cost of inaction.

## Phase 3: Solution · 180s
<!-- phase_id: 3, planned_duration_seconds: 180 -->

### Beat: Solution Intro
<!-- beat_id: 3_intro, tags: [critical] -->
Name and frame the solution in one clear sentence.
Counts as covered when the speaker states what the solution is.

### Beat: Demo
<!-- beat_id: 3_demo, tags: [critical] -->
Show it working. Live demo, screenshot, or concrete walkthrough.
Counts as covered when a specific example is shown or described in detail.

## Phase 4: Close · 120s
<!-- phase_id: 4, planned_duration_seconds: 120 -->

### Beat: Call to Action
<!-- beat_id: 4_cta, tags: [critical] -->
Tell the audience exactly what to do next. One clear action.
Counts as covered when the speaker gives a specific, actionable next step.

### Beat: Closer
<!-- beat_id: 4_close, tags: [critical, joke] -->
End on a high note — callback to the opening or a bold final statement.
Counts as covered when the speaker delivers a strong closing line.
